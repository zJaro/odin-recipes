THE PERFECT PIZZA DOUGH by Vito Iacopelli!

Description: Best Neapoltan styl pizza ever!!

Ingredients

POOLISH:

  • 300 ml Lukewar Water
  • 5 g Dry Yeast
  • 5 g Honey
  • 300 g 00 Flour

DOUGH

  • 700 ml Lukewarm Water
  • 40 g Salt
  • ,250 g 00 Flour

Steps for Poolish

  1. Mix together all ingredients
  2. Cover and set at room temperature for 1-hour
  3. After rest place overnight in fridge

Steps for Dough

  1. NOTE: Remove Poolish from frig 30 to 45 min to bring to room temperature. In a large bowl, add the Poolish, flour and all of the salt. IMPORANT: Add water a little at a time. DO NOT add all at once. Flour needs to absorb water little by little before adding more.
  2. Using a Kitchenaid stand mixer with dough hook, mix for 8 to 10 minutes; or hand knead for 10+ mins---dough will be sticky but together.
  3. Remove bowl and keeping top on top always, using bench scraper work dough with hands kinda like a big ball, with fingers underneath lifting dough off board, repeat. etc.
